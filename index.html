Accessible University

A tale of two sites. One version is inaccessible and one that has been remediated.

  • Before you (with accessibility problems)
  • After you (fixed)

Accessible University (AU) is a fictional university home page designed to demonstrate a variety of common web design problems that result in visitors with disabilities being unable to access content or features. AU was originally developed by AccessIT and is maintained by AccessComputing, both projects based out of the University of Washington.

This version of the Accessible University site, by Duke University, includes updates to the look, feel, and code. It has been adapted to use the Twitter Bootstrap framework since many components in the Bootstrap framework provide a quality accessible base to work from.

Use the AU site to:

  1. Demonstrate common web accessibility principles at trainings, presentations, and workshops on accessible web design.
  2. Learn common web accessibility problems and solutions in an easy-to-understand way.
